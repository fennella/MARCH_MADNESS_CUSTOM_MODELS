Advanced Stats

Pace Factor (PACE)
0
? An estimate of the number of possessions per team per 40 minutes. Formula is: 40 * (POSS / (0.2 * Minutes Played))
True Shooting % (TS)
0
? Measure of Shooting Efficiency that takes into account field goals, 3-point field goals and free throwss. Formula is: Points / (2 * (Field Goal Atttempts + 0.475 * Free Throw Attempts))
Effective Field Goal % (eFG)
0
? Measure of shooting efficiency adjusted for the fact that a 3-point field goal is worth one more point than a 2-point field goal. Formula is: (Field Goal + 0.5 * 3-Pointers) / Field Goal Attempts
Offensive Rating (ORtg)
0
? Points Scored Per 100 Possessions. Formula is: 100 * (Points / Possessions)
Defensive Rating (DRtg)
0
? Points Allowed Per 100 Possessions. Formula is: 100 * (Opponent Points / Possessions)
Net Rating (NRtg)
0
? Point Differential Per 100 Possessions. Formula is: 100 * ((Points - Opponent Points) / Possessions)
Free Throw Rate (FTr)
0
? A measure of percentage of shots taken are free throws. Formula is: Free Throw Attempts / Field Goal Attempts
Turnover Rate (TOVr)
0
? Estimated Turnovers Per 100 Plays. Formula is: 100 * Turnovers / (Field Goal Attempts + 0.475 * Free Throw Attempts + Turnovers)
Defensive Margin
0
? Measure of defensive in comparison to offensive points scored. Formula is: Points Allowed / (Points Allowed + Points Scored)
Offensive Rebound % (ORB)
0
? Measure of a team's offensive rebounds in comparison to their opponents. Formula is: Offensive Rebounds / (Offensive Rebounds + Opponent's Offensive Rebounds)
Effective Rebound % (ERB)
0
? Measure of a team's total rebounds in comparison to their opponents. Formula is: Total Rebounds / (Total Rebounds + Opponent Total Rebounds)
Block Percentage
0
? Percentage of all opponent field goal attempts are blocked
